In the past we loved SharePoint Subsites but now the modern way is to get rid of all your subsites.

### Advantages of a Subsite
1. Can be created by anyone with at least Create Subsites permission on the parent site
2. Can be automatically added to the navigation of the parent site
3. Inherits permissions and features automatically from the parent site

### Disadvantages of a Subsite
1. If you built your subsite structure to mimic your organisation structure and something changes then it's very difficult to alter the structure
2. A subsite shares the storage limitation of the parent site
3. URL's can get very long if you have nested sites

### Advantages of a Hubsite
1. Provisioning a new site collection for the Hubsite can be limited to a select group of individuals
2. Permissions can be unique for each Site Collection
3. Features can be unique just for a single Site Collection
4. Storage is allocated to each Site Collection

### Disadvantages of a Hubsite
1. Smaller companies may find it difficult to manage multiple site collections
